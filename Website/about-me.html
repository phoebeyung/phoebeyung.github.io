ABOUT ME

My passion, knowledge, and experience lie within water resource engineering through various co-op and work experiences as a Junior Water Resource Engineer. I have spent the past 3 years working across different areas of water resource, including analysis and design of water distribution systems, design of collection systems (storm and sanitary), approaches to stormwater management (BMP’s, LID, etc.), and urban hydrologic modeling/design. I have developed site-wide and local area water balances for multiple developments; assessed water management infrastructure; and designed water diversions and control structures such as bioretention cells, infiltration trench, and downspout disconnect.

Outside of work you’ll probably find me visiting hiking trails and parks, putting together a music playlist, or petting cats and dogs.
